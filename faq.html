Frequently Asked Questions

🧴 Products & Ingredients

Our soaps are made with 90%+ natural, high-quality ingredients including organic oils (olive, coconut, palm), shea butter, essential oils, natural colorants from herbs and clays, and distilled water. We use minimal artificial scents for fragrance, but never harsh chemicals like sulfates or parabens.

Most of our soaps are vegan-friendly. The Honey Oat soap contains honey which is not suitable for vegans. All other products in our range are plant-based. Please check individual product descriptions or contact us if you have specific concerns.

Yes! Our soaps are gentle and free from harsh chemicals, making them suitable for most sensitive skin types. We recommend our Aloe Vera Bliss and Honey Oat soaps for sensitive skin. However, if you have specific allergies, please check the ingredients list or do a patch test first.

With regular daily use, our soaps typically last 4-6 weeks. To maximize the life of your soap, keep it dry between uses by using a draining soap dish and storing it away from direct water spray. Unopened soaps have a shelf life of about 12 months.

Absolutely not! Bint-e-Samin Soaps is completely cruelty-free. We never test our products on animals, and we only use ingredients from suppliers who share our commitment to ethical practices.
